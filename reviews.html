Little Taco Shop Reviews

Loved the crunchy tacos

Rating: * * * * *

Update rating:

Favorite dish: Crunchy Tacos

Review: If you’re a fan of crunchy tacos, this place is a must-visit! I’ve had crunchy tacos from all over, but none compare to the ones here. The shell is perfectly crisp without being too hard, and it holds together with every bite. Every bite had that satisfying crunch, and I couldn’t get enough!


Best tacos I've had in a while

Rating: * * * * *

Update rating:

Favorite dish: Soft Tacos

Review: I've tried many taco spots around town, but these are hands down the best tacos I've had in a while! The flavors are incredible, the tortillas are fresh, and the meat is cooked to perfection. Definitely my new go-to taco place!
